Pasta Pesto with Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 340g pasta
  • 1-2 tablespoons pasta cooking water
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (sliced or cubed)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup basil pesto
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Preparation Time:

  • 30 minutes

Creamy, Flavor-Packed Pasta Pesto with Chicken

This Pasta Pesto with Chicken is a satisfying, vibrant dish that brings together the richness of basil pesto and the hearty goodness of tender, pan-seared chicken. Tossed with perfectly cooked pasta and finished with a sprinkle of Parmesan, it’s a well-balanced meal that feels both comforting and fresh. The pesto infuses every bite with herby, garlicky flavor, while the chicken adds a protein boost that makes it more filling than your average pasta dish.

Ideal for busy weeknights or casual dinners, this recipe comes together quickly and can be easily customized with your favorite vegetables or toppings. Whether you use store-bought pesto for convenience or make your own from scratch, the result is a crowd-pleasing, restaurant-worthy meal with minimal effort. Pair it with a simple salad or crusty bread for a complete, delicious dinner.

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to package directions until cooked. Reserve about ½ cup of the pasta water, then drain and set pasta aside.
  2. Cook the chicken: While the pasta cooks, season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook for about 5–7 minutes, or until golden and fully cooked through. Remove from heat.
  3. Combine pasta and pesto: Return the drained pasta to the pot or a large pan. Add the pesto and stir gently to coat the pasta evenly. If the sauce is too thick, stir in a tablespoon or two of reserved pasta water to loosen it.
  4. Add chicken and cheese: Stir in the cooked chicken and Parmesan cheese. Mix until everything is well combined and heated through.
  5. Divide among plates or bowls and top with extra Parmesan, fresh basil, or a drizzle of olive oil if desired. Optional: Garnish with cherry tomatoes or a handful of fresh baby spinach for extra color and flavor.
